Privacy Policy

At Skylens (https://skylens.likesyou.org/), we respect your privacy.

Information We Collect

  • Non-personal usage data (e.g., browser type, access time) that may be collected by hosting analytics.
  • Weather data fetched from public APIs (e.g., Open-Meteo). This data is not about you as a person.

With your permission, the app may use your device location to show local weather. Location data is used only to fetch the forecast and is not stored by us.

Ads
